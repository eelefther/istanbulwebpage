Suleymaniye Mosque

The Süleymaniye Mosque is an Ottoman imperial mosque located on the Third Hill of Istanbul, Turkey. It is the largest mosque in the city and one of the best-known sights of Istanbul. The mosque was built by the famous Ottoman architect Mimar Sinan between 1550 and 1557, during the reign of Sultan Suleiman the Magnificent.

The mosque is a masterpiece of Ottoman architecture and is considered one of the most beautiful mosques in the world. The Süleymaniye Mosque is a must-visit for anyone who wants to experience the beauty and grandeur of Ottoman architecture. The mosque is located on the Third Hill of Istanbul, overlooking the Golden Horn and the Bosphorus.
