Anonymous Transportation Card: Istanbulcard

For travelers, Istanbul offers an anonymous transportation card, valid for metro, buses, ferries, and trams. It's a convenient way to pay for rides without buying individual tickets. Prices:

  • Regular: 20 TL per ride
  • Student: 9.76 TL per ride
